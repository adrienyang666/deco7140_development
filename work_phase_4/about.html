About CCI Community Hub

We connect, empower, and celebrate our diverse community through meaningful engagement and inclusive initiatives.

Our hub welcomes people of all backgrounds, ages, and abilities. Accessibility and respect are at the heart of everything we do.

Our Mission

To foster a sense of belonging and growth for all community members by offering programs, events, and resources that reflect our values of inclusivity, respect, and collaboration.

Our Team

The CCI team includes passionate staff and volunteers from many different backgrounds. We are united in our dedication to building a safe, welcoming, and vibrant environment for everyone—no matter their journey or identity.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: Everyone is welcome at CCI—our programs are designed for all ages, cultures, and abilities.
  • Respect: We celebrate and respect our differences, and ensure every voice is heard.
  • Empowerment: We support each member to grow and thrive, providing resources and encouragement.
  • Collaboration: Together, we create a stronger, more connected community.
  • Accessibility: Our spaces and events are made as accessible as possible for everyone.
