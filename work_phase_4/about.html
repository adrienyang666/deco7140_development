About CCI

We connect, empower, and celebrate our diverse community through meaningful engagement and inclusive initiatives.

Our Mission

To foster a sense of belonging and growth for all community members by offering programs, events, and resources that reflect our values of inclusivity, respect, and collaboration.

Our Team

The CCI team is made up of passionate staff and volunteers from diverse backgrounds, all dedicated to building a welcoming environment for everyone.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: Everyone is welcome at CCI.
  • Respect: We celebrate and respect our differences.
  • Empowerment: We support every member to grow and thrive.
  • Collaboration: Together, we make our community stronger.
