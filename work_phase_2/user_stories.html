üìåSummary of Key User Stories

Based on our user research with three diverse personas from the Food Communities, we have identified the following actionable user stories:

  • 1. As a Chinese grandmother, I want to share my traditional recipes online so that my cultural knowledge can be preserved for future generations.
  • 2. As a multicultural chef, I want to promote fusion food events and upload visual content so I can reach wider audiences and engage more meaningfully.
  • 3. As an international student, I want to find and share comforting home recipes so that I feel emotionally connected and supported while living abroad.

These stories highlight the need for features such as audio and image uploading, multilingual support, community storytelling sections, and responsive design. These will directly inform our interface design and content structure.
