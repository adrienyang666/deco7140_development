Adrien's Client Needs Analysis

The Client Brief

About Us

We are a non-profit organisation dedicated to fostering social cohesion by empowering diverse communities to share their stories, connect with others, and access resources that enhance their visibility and influence.

This brief has been prepared for the internal design teams at Connect Communities Initiative (CCI). Each design team is tasked with developing individual concepts and prototypes for dynamic websites that align with CCI’s mission and vision.

Project Overview

We are commissioning the development of dynamic, user-friendly websites that represent and support different communities. Each website should serve as a central hub where community members can share resources, highlight events, and foster meaningful connections.

The websites must be accessible, mobile-friendly, and reflect the diverse needs of the selected community focus area while adhering to our mission of inclusivity, accessibility, and ethical design.

Community Focus Areas

  • Cultural and Heritage Communities
  • Academic and Professional Communities
  • Wellness and Mental Health Communities
  • Social and Environmental Advocacy Communities
  • Regional or International Communities
  • Creative Arts and Media Communities
  • Lived Experience Communities
  • Food and Culinary Communities
  • Tech and Innovation Communities

Target Audience & Goals

Audience:

  1. Community Members: Seeking connection, resources, and voice.
  2. General Public: Gaining awareness and appreciation of community contributions.

Goals:

  1. Celebrate authentic community identity (no stereotypes).
  2. Provide accessible, inclusive, clear information.
  3. Foster strong internal community support.
  4. Design ethically and culturally sensitively.

Based on the brief from Connect Communities Initiative (CCI), this website must reflect values of inclusivity, accessibility, and cultural sensitivity while empowering users through storytelling and digital participation.

Values, Needs, and Features

The following analysis outlines the key values of the client (CCI), the corresponding needs of the target users, and suggested website features. These elements ensure the final platform is welcoming, ethical, and community-driven.

Inclusivity

The client values inclusivity by ensuring all users feel welcome and represented—regardless of language, age, or background.

Need

Users need access to content in their native language.

Feature

Multi-language toggle between English and local languages.

Community Connection

CCI aims to create social bonds between users by enabling content sharing and collaboration.

Need

Users need a space to share experiences and stories.

Feature

A shared storytelling section for uploading personal stories.

Representation

CCI promotes authentic and respectful representation, ensuring that cultural narratives are expressed truthfully and free from stereotypes.

Need

Users want their culture to be reflected with dignity and accuracy.

Feature

Curated multimedia sections featuring voice, photo, and written submissions.
