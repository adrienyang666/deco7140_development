Introduction

This ethics analysis evaluates the website design through the lens of the Australian Computer Society (ACS) Code of Ethics. It focuses on ensuring that the design respects user dignity, protects cultural integrity, promotes inclusivity, and acknowledges the responsible use of AI tools.

1. Respect for Diversity and Cultural Sensitivity

The website supports cultural representation by enabling users from different backgrounds to share traditional recipes. Care is taken to avoid stereotypes and misrepresentation of food practices. Recipe submissions are community-moderated to ensure respectful content.

2. Privacy and Consent

When users submit stories or media, clear privacy notices and consent options are provided. No personal data is collected without permission. All user-contributed content is stored securely and users can request removal.

3. Accessibility and Inclusion

The website follows inclusive design practices, providing features like language toggles, screen reader compatibility, and readable text. This ensures users with different abilities or language proficiencies can engage equally.

4. Responsible Use of Generative AI

AI was used to simulate user personas and generate initial content ideas. All AI outputs are reviewed and edited by a human. The use of AI is acknowledged transparently and follows university integrity guidelines.

5. Alignment with ACS Code of Ethics

The design aligns with the following ACS ethical principles:

  • Primacy of the public interest: The design prioritises user well-being over commercial gain.
  • Enhancement of quality of life: The platform fosters connection and cultural celebration.
  • Honesty and transparency: AI use and privacy practices are disclosed clearly.

Conclusion

The ethical considerations incorporated in the design process ensure the website is not only functional, but also fair, inclusive, and socially responsible. It celebrates cultural diversity while protecting the rights and dignity of all users.
