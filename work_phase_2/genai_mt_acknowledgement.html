GenAI and Machine Translation Acknowledgement

1. Tools Used

I used Generative AI (ChatGPT) to support content generation for the five project pages in Work Phase 2. AI was used to simulate user interviews, draft content structures, check grammar, and suggest phrasing. I also used DeepL and Google Translate to assist in translating certain sections for bilingual drafts.

2. Modification and Critical Engagement

All AI-generated or translated content was carefully reviewed, edited, and restructured by me. I ensured that outputs aligned with the assignment requirements and reflected my own design decisions and understanding. No AI content was used “as-is” without thoughtful modification.

3. Benefits

These tools helped me save time, overcome language barriers, and explore more precise ways to express ideas. They were especially helpful when brainstorming user personas, writing accessible descriptions, and generating ethical language.

4. Risks and Ethical Reflection

I am aware that AI-generated content can contain bias, inaccuracies, or generic ideas. Relying too much on these tools without critical thinking could result in unoriginal work or academic integrity issues. I used them responsibly, with proper editing and reflection.

5. Transparency and Academic Integrity

This page serves as a full declaration of how I used GenAI and MT tools. I confirm that I complied with the course’s academic integrity guidelines and did not use any AI tools for unauthorised assistance or plagiarism.
